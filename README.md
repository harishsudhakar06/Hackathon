# Local Business Support Platform

## 📌 Project Overview
The Local Business Support Platform is a web application designed to help local businesses promote and sell their products online. Many small shop owners struggle to reach customers because they do not have an online presence. This platform connects local vendors with nearby customers through a simple digital marketplace.

The goal of this project is to support small businesses, increase their visibility, and encourage people to shop locally.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Support local and small businesses
- Provide an online platform for local vendors
- Help customers discover nearby shops
- Promote local economic growth

---

## 🚀 Features
- Vendor registration
- Product listing and management
- Customer product browsing
- Add to cart functionality
- Simple and user-friendly interface
